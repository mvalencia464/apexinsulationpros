## 1. Problem-Based Service Expansion

Instead of just listing services, frame them as solutions to common Florida homeowner "pain points."

### **The "Uncontrollable Power Bill" Fix**

- **Problem:** High energy costs caused by HVAC systems working overtime to fight the Florida humidity.
- **The Solution:** **Spray Foam Insulation & Radiant Barrier.**
  - *Enhanced Pitch:* Explain how sealing the "envelope" of the home keeps the attic within **10°F** of the living space, rather than the standard **150°F+** Florida attic.

### **The "Dust & Allergy" Cleanse**

- **Problem:** Old, dirty blown-in insulation acts as a reservoir for dust mites, pollen, and rodent dander, which is then pulled into the living space.
- **The Solution:** **Professional Blown-In Removal & Sanitation.**
  - *Enhanced Pitch:* Frame this as a "Home Health Reset." Don't just remove it—sanitize the attic floor before installing new, clean materials.

### **The "Hot Spots" Correction**

- **Problem:** Rooms that never get cool, or "ice cream headache" temperature swings between floors.
- **The Solution:** **Targeted Attic & Wall Retrofitting.**
  - *Enhanced Pitch:* Focus on "Precision Filling." Use thermal imaging (if you offer it) to show customers exactly where their walls are leaking air.

### **The "New Build" Peace of Mind**

- **Problem:** Builders often use the "minimum code" insulation, leaving new homeowners with high bills from day one.
- **The Solution:** **New Construction Performance Packages.**
  - *Enhanced Pitch:* Partnering with owners during the build phase to ensure a "Lifetime Insulation Guarantee."
